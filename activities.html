Summary of our Facilities

Each moment within this domain overflows with adventurous spirit and enlivening power and wondrous feeling. You will find the perfect experience in the selection we curated because we planned multiple thrilling activities for all types of people seeking refreshing activities beyond their regular routines. Skimming through the white pathways beneath you will provide an invigorating Ice Hiking experience. You can chill out with swimming either by yourself or with friends in the Swimming Pool while taking advantage of the perfect opportunity for family bonding and relaxation. Motorbike Boating stands out for those who want to combine speed and water with open-water excitement and sensation. Every person will find an unforgettable experience that perfectly matches their desire. Spending memories with you will become eternal keepsakes for the rest of your years.

Swimming Pool

To invigorate the body, the best wild swimming, a wonderful long swim off of our swimming dock in our clean lake is a must.  Adjacent to the lake, the pool is part of a 5000 square foot, multi-level patio. 1 outdoor salt-water pool 20 feet X 40 feet - Open May Long Weekend until Labour Day Weekend (We will extend into end of September if day time air temperature is above 25'C for minimum 4 days in row, like it surprisingly happened in September 2016 and 2017) Pool Temp 82'F & 28'Chours 10am to 7pm.  (September times if open at all will be limited from 12 noon to 5 pm)
